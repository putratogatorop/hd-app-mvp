## Brand in one line
A small luxury, measured in spoonfuls. Editorial, wine-and-paper, bilingual ID/EN. Burgundy `#650A30` on cream `#FEF2E3`, display set in Cormorant Garamond with italic flourishes, body in Jost, numerals in JetBrains Mono. Sharp corners, hairline rules, no gradients on UI, no emoji in copy, icons are lucide-react at ~1.75 stroke.
